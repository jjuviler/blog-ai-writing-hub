Getting Started

Quick Start (5 Minutes)

Step 1: Watch the WRITE Framework Video

Start with Video #01: Intro to the WRITE Framework (5 min). This teaches you the foundational prompting methodology.

Step 2: Download Claude Skills

Visit the Claude Skills section and click "Download from Google Drive" to access all skills:

  • Alt Text Generator: For listicle posts with 20-100+ images
  • Brief QA: Catch hallucinated URLs before you start writing
  • Product Research Assistant: Research competitor products with verification flags

Step 3: Install Your First Skill

  1. Go to claude.ai → Settings → Capabilities → Skills
  2. Drag and drop one of the .zip files into the upload area
  3. Enable the skill in your skills list
  4. Watch Video #03 to see it in action

Recommended Learning Path

Week 1: Foundations

  • Learn the WRITE Framework
  • Install and test Brief QA on your next brief
  • Practice structured prompting on 2–3 blog outlines

Week 2: Skill Integration

  • Use Brief QA at the start of every brief
  • Install Alt Text Generator for your next listicle
  • Watch Video #02 on creating your own skills

Week 3: Advanced Workflows

  • Try Product Research Assistant for competitor analysis
  • Create your first custom skill based on a workflow you've perfected
  • Share what you've learned with a teammate
