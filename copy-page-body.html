Copy Page Body

Download Extension

Overview

Copy Page Body is a Chrome extension that copies a HubSpot blog post's content to your clipboard with a single click. It only works on HubSpot blog posts.

Installation

  1. Click Download Extension above and unzip the downloaded file.
  2. Go to chrome://extensions/ in your browser.
  3. Turn on Developer mode using the toggle in the top right corner.
  4. Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder.

Usage

Navigate to any HubSpot blog post, then click the Copy Page Body extension icon in your Chrome toolbar. The post's content will be copied to your clipboard instantly.
