HubSpot Products

This skill is shared with all HubSpot blog writers — you should have access automatically. To activate it, go to Customize → Skills in Claude and look for it under Shared Skills. Select the skill, then toggle it on in the top right. If you don't see the skill, reach out to your manager.

Overview

The HubSpot Products skill provides a comprehensive database of HubSpot's product pages, pricing tiers, features, and integrations. It's designed to be your source of truth for accurate HubSpot product information when writing blog content.

Why It Exists: HubSpot's live product pages are often hard for AI to read due to JavaScript rendering, cookie banners, and complex page structures. This skill provides clean, LLM-optimized product data.

What It Covers

  • All 8 HubSpot Hubs: Marketing, Sales, Service, CMS, Operations, Commerce, Payments, Data Management
  • Pricing Information: Free, Starter, Professional, Enterprise tiers for each hub
  • Feature Details: Specific capabilities and tools within each hub
  • Integrations: Connected apps and platform compatibility
  • Product Relationships: How different hubs and features work together

When to Use

  • Writing sections about specific HubSpot features (lead scoring, email automation, etc.)
  • Comparing HubSpot pricing tiers in listicles or buying guides
  • Fetching accurate product descriptions for blog intros or context sections
  • Verifying feature availability across different hub tiers
  • Any time you need canonical HubSpot product information

How to Use

Example 1: Writing About a Specific Feature

Write a paragraph about HubSpot's lead scoring feature in Marketing Hub.
Use the HubSpot Products skill for accurate information.

Example 2: Fetching Pricing Information

What's the pricing for each tier of Marketing Hub?
Use the HubSpot Products skill.

Important Notes

Data Freshness

  • Current as of: April 2026 (snapshot of live HubSpot product pages)
  • Update schedule: Quarterly (after major launches like INBOUND, Spotlight)
  • Always verify: For posts about brand-new features or recent pricing changes, check live pages

What This Prevents

  • Hallucinated features: Claude won't make up product capabilities
  • Outdated pricing: Gets pricing from curated data, not random web results
  • Wrong hub assignments: Knows which features belong to which hub
  • Broken links: Provides canonical URLs that actually work
