ReCraft

Overview

ReCraft is an AI image generation tool designed for professional and brand-consistent visual content. It supports a range of styles and gives users fine-grained control over image style, color palette, and composition. ReCraft is built with design workflows in mind, making it well-suited for producing polished, on-brand visuals at scale.

The HubSpot Blog uses ReCraft to generate featured images for posts. Using a shared account with consistent style settings ensures that featured images maintain a uniform look and feel across the blog, reinforcing brand consistency and standard of quality.

Getting Access

ReCraft is available at recraft.ai. The Blog team uses a shared account — log in with the email jjuviler@hubspot.com.

If ReCraft prompts you to enter a verification code, reach out to @jjuviler on Slack and ask him to send you the code. Once you enter it, you'll be logged in and ready to use the tool.
